Vestra Bootstrap Launch — Budget Edition

First-project, minimum-cash launch plan. Founder does most work. Free tiers everywhere. Skip enterprise-grade until revenue justifies. Prepared 2026-07-04.

Bottom line — bootstrap mode.
Upfront (0-3 months, first customer live): ~KES 180,000 (~USD 1,400).
Recurring infra (no salary): ~KES 8,000-12,000/mo (~USD 60-90).
Optional survival salary for founder (Nairobi): KES 80,000-120,000/mo.
6-month total burn (solo founder + tiny salary): KES 700k-1.2M (USD 5,400-9,200).
The core budget move. Skip escrow for launch. Launch as a verified-listings + rent-collection-passthrough platform. Escrow needs a bank trust deed (KES 500k-1M + 3-6 months). Add it in Phase 2 once revenue proves the product. Charge landlords KES 1,000 per verified listing and 1% of rent collected — same value prop, no trust account required.

1. The bootstrap thesis

Ship a lean MVP that Kenyan landlords and tenants pay for on day one, using free-tier infrastructure and founder sweat equity. Every expensive gate (bank trust, DPO retainer, insurance, trademark, KYC vendor, marketing spend) is deferred until revenue clears the bar. Two products in v1:

  • Paid verified listings for landlords + agents. KES 1,000 per 30-day listing, KES 3,000 featured. Founder verifies each listing manually (Ardhisasa search + phone-call landlord identity check).
  • Rent collection as pass-through. Tenant pays via M-Pesa Paybill; funds route straight to landlord bank account after Vestra's 1% cut. Not escrow — just a rail. Launch this without a trust account.

Break-even math: 150 listings/mo at KES 1,000 = KES 150k/mo covers survival + infra. Achievable by month 4-5 with organic marketing.

3. What you CANNOT skip (legal walls)

Hard walls — non-negotiable even in bootstrap.
  • Register the company (private limited) — KES 10,650 via eCitizen BRS. No paying customer without it.
  • Get corporate KRA PIN — free but mandatory to receive money into a business account.
  • ODPC registration as controller + processor — KES 4,000-8,000. Legal from user #1.
  • Publish Terms of Service + Privacy Policy before first user signup — advocate one-off drafting KES 60-120k for bundle.
  • Never hold escrow funds without partner-bank trust — keep rent as pass-through only in v1. Vestra's cut clears within the day; no funds held overnight.
  • Register a business bank account (not personal) to receive company revenue.
  • File nil returns even if pre-revenue — KRA penalty is KES 2,000/return for skipping.

6. What to build first (v1 scope)

Rule. If a feature does not help you charge a landlord KES 1,000 this month, cut it from v1.

Must-ship (weeks 1-8)

  1. Landlord signup + KRA PIN + phone verification (SMS OTP via Africa's Talking)
  2. Property listing creation (title, LR number, price, photos, geolocation from phone GPS)
  3. Founder-manual verification queue: check Ardhisasa search + phone-call landlord
  4. Public listing page + WhatsApp lead capture (tenant clicks → prefilled WA message to landlord + Vestra)
  5. Landlord dashboard: listings + leads + payment history
  6. Payment via IntaSend shared Paybill (KES 1,000 = 30-day listing, KES 3,000 = featured)
  7. Simple admin dashboard for founder
  8. ToS + Privacy + Cookie consent banner
  9. Password reset via Resend email

Ship if time (weeks 9-16)

  1. Tenant signup + save favourites
  2. Basic messaging (in-app + email notification)
  3. Rent collection pass-through (tenant pays via IntaSend Paybill, Vestra takes 1%, forwards rest to landlord next-day)
  4. Landlord monthly rent report (CSV export for KRA MRI self-filing)
  5. Capacitor Android build (skip iOS in v1 — no EAS subscription needed)

Do NOT build in v1

  • Escrow (needs bank trust)
  • Full KYC vendor integration (manual Huduma ID review)
  • Geofenced photo verification (founder physically visits first 20 listings)
  • AI concierge (static FAQ page)
  • eRITS Gava Connect (landlord downloads MRI CSV → self-files)
  • eTIMS integration (defer until landlord asks)
  • Ardhisasa API workflow (manual search, screenshot to listing)
  • iOS app (Android + PWA cover 92% of KE mobile market)
  • Diaspora vertical
  • Chama features
  • Agent trust graph v2
  • Meilisearch / RAG search
  • Voice search
  • 3D tours

7. Bootstrap step-by-step (30 numbered actions)

Week 1 — company + accounts

1. Register company on eCitizen BRS. Pick 3 name alternatives ("Vestra", "Vestra Kenya", "Vestra Properties"). Submit memo + articles (BRS templates fine). KES 10,650. 5-14 working days.
2. While waiting, register domain vestra.co.ke via Truehost or Sasahost. KES 1,500/yr.
3. Set up Cloudflare account, add vestra.co.ke, enable Email Routing (free) → forward founder@vestra.co.ke to your Gmail.
4. Once BRS certificate received: apply for corporate KRA PIN via iTax. Same day.
5. Open Equity SME business account (or Family Bank / Co-op Bank). Digital onboarding. Same day.
6. Apply for Nairobi County single business permit. Category: technology / consultancy. KES 15,000. 3-7 days.

Week 2 — legal + data protection

7. Contact one solo KE advocate (LinkedIn "Kenya technology lawyer" or Sheria KE directory). Ask for a fixed-fee bundle: ToS + Privacy Policy + Cookie Notice + Refund Policy + Tenancy Template + Landlord Services Agreement. Aim for KES 60-100k bundle price. 1-2 week turnaround.
8. File ODPC registration as Controller AND Processor at odpc.go.ke. Documents needed: BRS certificate, KRA PIN, DPIA summary (write your own — 2 pages: what data, why, retention, subject rights), processing activities list. KES 8,000 total. 2-4 weeks.
9. Buy cyber liability micro-policy from Britam or APA online. KES 5M cover, targeted at SME tech. KES 25,000/yr.

Week 3-4 — tech setup

10. Provision Hetzner CX22 VPS (Falkenstein). Install Ubuntu 24.04, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Redis, Caddy (auto-HTTPS reverse proxy). EUR 5/mo.
11. Sign up: Neon (Postgres free), Sentry, Grafana Cloud, PostHog, OneSignal, Resend, BetterStack (uptime). All free tiers.
12. Sign up IntaSend, complete KYC (BRS + KRA PIN + directors' IDs), get sandbox + Paybill.
13. Sign up Africa's Talking, buy KES 5,000 SMS credit, apply for Sender ID "VESTRA" (3-5 days).
14. Deploy staging: Vestra backend on Hetzner, frontend on Cloudflare Pages, DB on Neon, R2 for images, Caddy TLS.
15. Apply Prisma baseline migration on staging DB (run prisma migrate deploy).

Weeks 5-8 — v1 build

16. Ship the 9 must-ship features listed above (§6).
17. Publish ToS + Privacy on vestra.co.ke/legal.
18. Post the ODPC-registered badge in footer once granted.

Weeks 9-12 — first customer

19. Manually source 20 verified listings (WhatsApp + friends + Kilimani + Kileleshwa landlord walk-in).
20. Charge KES 1,000 per listing. Take payment via IntaSend Paybill.
21. Launch to 3 Kenyan diaspora Facebook groups (US + UK) + 5 Nairobi renter WhatsApp groups. Zero paid ads.
22. First tenant lead → first WhatsApp connect → first paying landlord. Target: week 12.

Months 3-6 — traction phase

23. Set weekly SEO cadence: 1 suburb guide + 1 rent-market-report post per week. Rank on "houses for rent in [suburb]" long tail.
24. When rent-collection revenue hits KES 200k/mo, apply for own Daraja Paybill (still keep IntaSend as fallback).
25. Hire first part-time dev (KES 80-120k/mo) or offer equity + KES 40k stipend.
26. Approach HFC or NCBA for trust-account discovery meeting. Bring: 6 months revenue evidence, DPO commitment, ToS, DPIA, insurance certificate. Expect 3-6 months to trust deed signing.

Month 6-9 — trust rail + fundraise

27. Trust deed signed → launch escrow product v1 (sales only, rent stays pass-through).
28. Retain fractional DPO (KES 150k/mo). Upgrade insurance to include PI + D&O.
29. Draft pre-seed pitch deck. Approach Baobab Network, Antler East Africa, Founders Factory Africa.
30. File trademark at KIPI (KES 40-100k with self-filing or agent). Register Vestra Credit Ltd if planning Rent Advance.

9. Bootstrap risks + mitigations

ODPC delays. Application backlog can stretch 4-8 weeks. Mitigation: submit ODPC application day 1; launch to closed beta only (10 users you know personally) until certificate arrives.
Legal shortcut backfires. Cheap advocate ships weak ToS with no limitation of liability. Mitigation: use LawCadi templates as base, ask advocate for a 2-hour review and localization for KES 20-30k instead of full drafting.
Rent pass-through drift into escrow. If Vestra holds rent overnight while landlord provides bank details, it looks like escrow to CBK. Mitigation: automate next-day payout to landlord's bank via IntaSend PesaLink; document as pass-through processor, not custodian.
Founder burnout. Solo bootstrapping while doing on-site verification is a real risk. Mitigation: cap founder verifications at 40 listings/mo; hire a KES 30k/mo intern for verification-runs by month 3.
IntaSend outage. Single payment provider = single point of failure. Mitigation: add manual Buy-Goods number as fallback path in listing UI; move to Daraja direct by month 6.

13. The single most important rule

Every KES you spend before revenue = 30-90 days of runway lost.

Say NO to every vendor pitch until month 6. Say NO to hiring a co-founder before you have 20 paying landlords. Say NO to fancy design system before you have 100 tenants using the app.

The audit + strategy documents already produced (VESTRA_STRATEGY_2026.md + 5 research streams + full-plan checklist) tell you exactly what "good" looks like at scale. Ignore 80% of them for the first 6 months. Ship a scrappy verified-listings + rent-passthrough MVP, take money, then upgrade one component at a time as revenue clears each cost.
